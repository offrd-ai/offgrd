FAQ

Questions coaches ask.

Does it replace Hudl?

No — it rides on it. Keep your film in Hudl; export the breakdown and upload it to OFFGRD as-is (.xlsx or .csv, no conversion). Hudl stores what happened. OFFGRD tells you what's coming and teaches your roster what to do about it.

Does it really work without internet?

Yes. Predictions, the Caller, the playbook, and training all run on-device. The cloud is for syncing your program between coaches and players — when you're back on a connection, everything catches up automatically.

Who owns the playbook?

The program. Your plays live in your team's account with roles — Admin, Coach·Edit, Coach·View, Player — so the whole staff builds one system and every player studies the same library. Export your playbook as a file any time.

How do players join?

One join code. They sign up, tap "I'm a player", enter the code, pick their position, and start training. Coaches see every test score by player under Results.

What does the coach have to build before players can train?

Nothing. Reps Lab ships with built-in concepts versus six coverages, and the starter playbook is testable the moment you load it. Add your own plays and the tests grow with your system.
